About TAKNET-PS

TAKNET-PS is an independently developed project focused on delivering free, low-latency ADS-B data to public safety users worldwide.

The software, infrastructure, AI-assisted development tools, and deployed feeder hardware are personally funded and maintained. Significant time and resources go into keeping the network reliable, fast, and expanding into underserved regions.

Support directly enables:

  • Expansion of feeder coverage in areas with poor aviation visibility
  • Server and infrastructure upgrades
  • Continued feature development and performance optimization
  • Community-funded feeder deployments
